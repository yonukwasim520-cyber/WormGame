# 🐛 Worm Game Simulation

A worm simulation game where different worms interact, move, fight, eat, and survive in a dynamic environment.

## 📌 About The Project

Worm Game Simulation is a project that creates a small artificial ecosystem of worms.

Each worm has its own behavior, energy system, movement, and interactions with other worms. The simulation includes fighting, healing, food gathering, and survival mechanics.

The goal of the project is to create a simple but interactive world where worms behave automatically based on their conditions.

## ✨ Features

### 🐛 Worm System
- Multiple worms can exist at the same time.
- Each worm has a body made of multiple segments.
- Worms move smoothly around the map.
- Worms can belong to different teams.

### ⚔️ Combat System
- Different worm teams can fight each other.
- Worms attack nearby enemies.
- A worm loses energy during battles.
- Worms can die when their energy reaches zero.

### 🔵 Doctor Worm
- A special blue doctor worm.
- Searches for injured worms.
- Follows damaged worms.
- Heals worms when it gets close.

### 🍃 Food System
- Food can be added to the map.
- Worms collect food to restore energy.
- Food can be removed from the environment.

### 💀 Corpse System
- Dead worms remain in the world.
- Corpses slowly change over time.
- Living worms can eat corpses.
- Corpses are consumed piece by piece instead of disappearing instantly.

### 🔋 Energy System
- Every worm has an energy value.
- Energy decreases over time.
- Eating food restores energy.
- Energy can be customized.
- High energy values can create unlimited energy mode.

### 🎮 Controls
- Create different types of worms.
- Add and remove food.
- Pause and resume the simulation.
- Select worms and view their information.
- Modify worm energy.

### 🌍 Environment
- Circular world boundaries.
- Worms cannot leave the map.
- Automatic movement and behavior system.
